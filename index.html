Welcome to Thrifting in NYC: For Beginners!

I am introducing you to the wonderful world of thrift stores. It's very easy to find hidden gems, but you need to know where to look for them. Presently, this website will focus on clothes and non-profit stores in NYC.

I provide a brief summary of thrifting resources. I encorage you to visit the stores and their websites for their current policies.
